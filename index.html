Jiashun Wang

I am a second-year master student at UC, San Diego, advised by Prof. Xiaolong Wang. Previously, I received my B.S. degree in Statistics (Data Science) from Fudan University. I have worked with Prof. Yanwei Fu and Dr. Yinda Zhang.

My research interest lies at the intersection of Computer Vision and Robot Learning. Specifically, I am interested in 3D vision about human/robot/object/scene interaction. I have worked on projects modeling the visual human-human/object/scene interaction and recently I am working on how to provide this kind of human-environment prior knowledge to robot and help robot to learn to perform dexterous manipulation.
